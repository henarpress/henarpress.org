Support Kurdish Literature

Henar Press is dedicated to bringing Kurdish literature to English-speaking readers. As the largest stateless nation in the world, Kurds have faced censorship and oppression throughout the Kurdistan regions. By supporting our mission, you're helping to promote Kurdish writers.

Why We're Nonprofit

We chose to operate as a nonprofit organization to ensure that our focus remains on the cultural and literary value of our work, rather than commercial interests. This allows us to prioritize important Kurdish authors that might otherwise go unheard.

How You Can Help

Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a big impact on our mission to promote Kurdish literature. As a nonprofit run by a dedicated but small team, we face significant financial challenges, especially with the costly process of translating works. If you'd like to be part of our mission, you make a tax-deductible donation through our Give Lively page. Your generosity plays a crucial role in keeping Kurdish literature accessible to readers everywhere.

You can also support Kurdish authors by picking up their books from your local independent bookstore or ordering online through Bookshop.org. If buying isn't an option, consider borrowing from your local library. If you need some recommendations, take a look at our database of Kurdish literature in English!

Volunteer Opportunities

While we're not currently accepting interns, we welcome volunteers who can contribute their skills to our small team. If you have expertise in any of the following areas, we'd love to hear from you:

  • Publishing and Marketing
  • Graphic design
  • Illustration and book design
  • Typography and typesetting
  • Website design and development
  • Proofreading and copyediting

Even if you can only spare time for short consultation meetings, your expertise would be invaluable to us. Please reach out through our contact page.
