About Henar Press

Henar Press is a nonprofit independent publisher dedicated to bringing the rich tapestry of Kurdish literature to a global audience. Founded in 2022, we are passionate about translating and publishing novels, poetry, and memoirs that reflect the complexity and diversity of Kurdish culture.

Our name, "Henar," means pomegranate in Kurdish, symbolizing the sweet, tangy, and sometimes bitter stories we aim to share with the world. Like the many seeds within a pomegranate, Kurdish literature contains multitudes, and we are committed to nurturing and spreading these seeds of culture far and wide.

"Against Forgetting, We Write"

Our Mission

At Henar Press, we believe in the power of literature to bridge cultures, foster understanding, and preserve heritage. Our mission is threefold:

  1. To introduce English-speaking readers to the wealth of Kurdish literature, both classic and contemporary.
  2. To support Kurdish authors and translators, providing a platform for their voices to be heard.
  3. To contribute to the preservation and promotion of Kurdish language and culture through the written word.

Our Commitment

We are committed to ethical publishing practices, fair compensation for authors and translators, and the highest standards of literary quality. Henar Press operates as a nonprofit organization, reinvesting all proceeds into our mission of promoting Kurdish literature and supporting Kurdish writers.

As we continue to grow, we invite readers, writers, and literature enthusiasts from around the world to join us in this journey of cultural exchange and literary discovery. Together, we can ensure that the rich traditions and contemporary voices of Kurdish literature find their rightful place on the global stage.
