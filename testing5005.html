HENAR PRESS

Henar Press is an independent, Kurdish-owned nonprofit publisher based in Columbus, Ohio. We translate and publish experimental novels, poetry, and memoirs from emerging and established writers in the homeland and diaspora—challenging the reductive portrayal of Kurdistan as stateless.

PEOPLE

Aryan Omar Hassan

Publisher & Founder

Aryan is a fiction writer and literary designer based in Columbus. He has previously lived in Oslo, Stockholm, Sulaymaniyah, Abu Dhabi, and London, and holds degrees from The Ohio State University. He maintains the Kurdish literary database.

Hannah

Database Co-Developer

Hannah co-developed the foundational architecture for the Kurdish literary database, ensuring the preservation and accessibility of historical and contemporary texts across the diaspora.
