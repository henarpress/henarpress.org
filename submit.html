Kurdish Literary Database

We're compiling a collection of Kurdish literature in English. Entries must be literary works by Kurdish authors or those of Kurdish descent. This excludes literary criticism, historical articles, and academic essays, however important they may be. We accept both books and online publications (include links for online publications in the Notes section). Thank you for contributing to our database!
