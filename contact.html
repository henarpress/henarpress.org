Manuscript Submissions

Henar Press is interested in publishing a wide range of genres, including novels, novellas, memoirs, poetry, chapbooks, and hybrid forms. If your text is not in English and you're looking for a translator and publisher, please contact us directly using the form below

Please follow these guidelines when submitting your manuscript:

  • Format your manuscript in a standard font (e.g., Times New Roman, 12pt) with double spacing.
  • Include a cover letter with a brief synopsis and author bio.
  • For prose submissions, please send the first 50 pages. For poetry, send 10-15 pages.
  • Save your submission as a PDF or Word document.

Please note that it may take some time until we reach a decision about your manuscript, but we will respond to every submission. We appreciate your patience during this process.
