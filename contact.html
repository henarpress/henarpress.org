Contact

We welcome your inquiries, manuscript submissions, and general feedback. Henar Press is interested in publishing a wide range of genres, including novels, novellas, short story collections, poetry, memoirs, narrative journalism, chapbooks, and hybrid forms. If your manuscript is not in English, please contact us directly using the form and we will reach out to you with more information.

Submission Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines when submitting your manuscript:

  • Format your manuscript in a standard font (e.g., Times New Roman, 12pt) with double spacing. Your manuscript can be in either Word or PDF format. Please limit file sizes to 5 MB.
  • Include a cover letter with a brief synopsis and author bio.
  • For prose submissions, please send the first 50 pages. For poetry, send 10-15 pages.

We offer a traditional publishing contract and arrangement. All book-related services are handled by our team at no cost to the author. Our team currently aims to publish a small number of books annually to give each publication the attention it deserves.


It will take some time until we reach a decision about your manuscript, but we respond to every submission.
